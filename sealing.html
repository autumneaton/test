What we Learned about the Sealing and Family

What Autumn Learned:

The sealing ordinance is the final culmination of uniting families. Without the sealing ordinance, families would not be together in the eternities. Going to the temple to do proxy sealings was also a wonderful opportunity because it is the only ordinance that is performed with multiple people making covenants to each other, together, with God, as a family.

What Conrad Learned:

This time was my first time doing proxy sealings in the temple. I was very humbled by the experience. I was able to be sealed for my great grandfather to his spouse. I felt his hapiness as he was able to be united to his love for all eternity. The ordinance taught me that there is no greater joy than being with your family and the people you love.
