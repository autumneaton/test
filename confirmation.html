What We Learned About The Gift of The Holy Ghost(Confirmation) and Family

What Autumn Learned:

Here on earth, fathers and brothers can bless their family by helping administer the Gift of the Holy Ghost to their family members. Without this ordinance, we will not have the power to keep our baptismal covenant and be cleansed. This means we cannot be with God or our families in the eternities.


What Conrad Learned:

The Gift of the Holy Ghost helps heal and repair families. While in the temple, I rememberd moments when the Holy Ghost reminded me to apologize for my anger, give resources to my family, and check in on siblings I had not seen in a while. Without such guidance from the Spirit of God, my family relationships would not be as intact and meaningful as they are today.

What is the Gift of The Holy Ghost?

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints teaches that the Gift of the Holy Ghost is a sacred ordinance that is necessary for salvation.
